🧾 How to Check Your Result (Step-by-Step)

  • 📲 Open the AKTU One View Portal (link above)
  • 🧑‍🎓 Enter your Roll Number
  • 📅 Select the correct Academic Session (e.g., 2024–25)
  • 🔍 Click on "Show Result"
  • 🖨️ Your marksheet will be displayed. Click "Print" or "Download PDF"

❓Forgot Your Roll Number?

  • 🔸 Check your Admit Card or AKTU ERP Dashboard
  • 🔸 Contact your college admin or exam department
  • 🔸 Still stuck? Call the AKTU Helpline

📊 What Does the Result Include?

  • Subject-wise Marks
  • Internal & External Scores
  • SGPA / CGPA
  • Pass/Fail Status
  • Back Paper (if any)

📌 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. What if my result is not showing?
👉 It might still be processing. Try again after 24 hours or contact your college.

Q2. What does ‘INC’ or ‘XX’ mean?
👉 Your internal marks or practicals are pending. Contact your department.

Q3. Can I apply for re-evaluation?
👉 Yes, AKTU will soon open the rechecking portal. Stay tuned to the official site.
