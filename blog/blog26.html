📘 AKTU One-Stop Guide: Syllabus, Result, Datesheet, ERP Login (2025)

Everything AKTU Students Need – in One Place!

🎓 1. AKTU Syllabus – Download Semester-wise

🔗 Download Syllabus (Official Link)

  • Choose Institute Type → Course → Branch → Semester
  • Click and download the syllabus PDF
  • Use updated version (2022/2025 regulation)

📅 2. AKTU Datesheet – Exam Timetable

🔗 AKTU Exam Datesheet

  • Visit “Examination Schedule” under “Latest Circulars”
  • Find your course-wise PDF (B.Tech, MBA, etc.)
  • Keep checking for revisions

📜 3. AKTU Result – One View Portal

🔗 Check Result Now

  • Enter Roll Number & Select Course
  • Click “Show Result”
  • For issues, contact your college admin

🔐 4. AKTU ERP Login – Admit Card, Marks, Forms

🔗 Visit ERP Portal

  • Click “Student Login”
  • Enter ERP ID & Password
  • Access Admit Cards, Internal Marks, Exam Forms
  • Best viewed on Google Chrome

📚 5. What You Can Do on ERP:

Feature Description
🧾 Admit Card Download before semester exams
📈 View Internal Marks Sessional + Practical marks
📝 Exam Forms Fill for regular/carry-over exams
🎓 Graduation Portal Apply for degree post final year
📋 College Info View attendance/faculty details

💬 FAQs

Q1: ERP Login not working?
👉 Try incognito mode or clear browser cache. If issue persists, contact college ERP coordinator.

Q2: Where can I see revaluation results?
👉 Revaluation results are updated on the One View portal.

Q3: What is AKTU semester schedule?
👉 Typically:
• Odd Sem (1,3,5,7): Dec–Jan
• Even Sem (2,4,6,8): May–June
