AKTU B.Tech CSE Semester-wise Syllabus [2025 Updated]

Your complete guide to semester subjects, labs & project planning.

Program Overview

  • Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
  • Structure: Core CSE + engineering fundamentals + labs + projects
  • Assessment: Theory exams, internals, practicals, project work
  • System: CBCS (Choice Based Credit System)

Semester Wise Syllabus

✅ 1st Semester (Common)

  • Engineering Mathematics – I
  • Engineering Physics
  • Engineering Chemistry / Environmental Science
  • Basic Electrical Engineering
  • Communication Skills
  • Engineering Mechanics

Labs: Physics/Chem Lab • Electrical Eng Lab • Workshop • Language Lab

✅ 2nd Semester (Common)

  • Engineering Mathematics – II
  • Basic Electronics Engineering
  • Engineering Graphics / Programming Problem Solving
  • Engineering Geology / Emerging Tech
  • Thermodynamics / Engineering Chemistry

Labs: Programming, Electronics, Graphics Labs

✅ 3rd Semester (CSE Core)

  • Data Structures
  • Digital Logic Design
  • Discrete Structures
  • OOP using C++
  • Computer Organization & Architecture

Labs: Data Structures Lab • OOP Lab • DLD Lab

✅ 4th Semester

  • Design & Analysis of Algorithms
  • Operating Systems
  • Microprocessors & Interfacing
  • Database Management Systems (DBMS)
  • Software Engineering

Labs: DAA Lab • OS Lab • DBMS Lab

✅ 5th Semester

  • Computer Networks
  • Theory of Computation (TOC)
  • Compiler Design
  • Web Technology
  • Industrial Training/Internship

Labs: CN Lab • Web Lab • Compiler Lab

✅ 6th Semester

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
  • Software Testing
  • Mobile App Development
  • Minor Project / Seminar

Labs: AI/ML Lab • Mobile App Lab • Testing Lab

✅ 7th Semester

  • Data Science & Big Data Analytics
  • Information & Cyber Security
  • Elective–1 (Cloud Computing / IoT)
  • Major Project – Phase I

✅ 8th Semester

  • Blockchain / Advanced Elective
  • Industrial Project – Phase II
  • Internship + Viva Voce

How to Download PDF

You can view or download the official syllabus from the AKTU website:

  • Select Program: B.Tech → Branch: Computer Science & Engineering → Regulation: 2025

Final Tips

  • Keep a printed copy of the syllabus for tracking.
  • Highlight high-weightage & frequently asked units.
  • Pair syllabus tracking with PYQs for better preparation.
  • Visit our Study Material section for notes, PYQs, and lab manuals.
