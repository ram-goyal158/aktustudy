🎫 AKTU Admit Card 2025 – How to Download from ERP Portal

Step-by-step guide for B.Tech, MBA, MCA, and all AKTU students

🧾 Who Can Download the Admit Card?

  • ✅ B.Tech (All Semesters & Branches)
  • ✅ B.Pharma
  • ✅ MBA, MCA, BBA
  • ✅ Regular & Back Paper Students
  • ✅ Students with No Fee Dues or Attendance Issues

📌 Admit card is mandatory for entry to the exam hall.

🔗 Official Portal to Download Admit Card

🖥️ ERP Portal: https://erp.aktu.ac.in

🖱️ How to Download AKTU Admit Card – Step-by-Step

  1. Go to: erp.aktu.ac.in
  2. Click on “Student Login”
  3. Enter:
    • User ID (your University Roll No)
    • Password (default/college provided)
  4. After login, go to “Admit Card” section
  5. Select semester/year
  6. Click “Download Admit Card”

✅ Save and print at least 2 copies for safety.

🔍 Details to Check on Admit Card

  • ✅ Student Name, Roll No, Branch, Semester
  • ✅ Exam Center Name & Code
  • ✅ Subject Codes & Exam Dates
  • ✅ Exam Shift: Morning / Evening
  • ✅ Photograph & Signature
  • ✅ Instructions (e.g., COVID guidelines, dress code)

🧾 Forgot ERP Password?

  • Click “Forgot Password” on ERP login
  • Use registered mobile/email
  • Or contact college’s ERP coordinator for help

🛑 Common Issues & Solutions

Issue Possible Solution
Login Failed Check your credentials carefully
Admit Card Not Showing Ensure fee is paid and exam form is submitted
Wrong Subjects Listed Contact your college exam cell ASAP
Server Not Responding Try again in non-peak hours (early morning)

📅 Exam Day Checklist

  • 🖨️ Print Admit Card (2 copies)
  • 🎓 Carry College ID Card
  • 🕓 Reach 30–45 minutes before exam time
  • ✅ Carry black/blue pens, sanitizer, mask
