📝 Best Apps for AKTU Students – Notes, Revision & Reminders (2025)

Stay organized, reduce stress & improve your focus using the right study apps!

🧠 Notion – All-in-One Productivity App

🔗 Download on Android/iOS/Web

  • Custom class-wise notebooks
  • To-do lists, planners, images & links in one dashboard
  • Perfect for managing group projects
✅ Best for ultra-organized students

✍️ Microsoft OneNote – Digital Notebook

🔗 Download for Android/iOS/Desktop

  • Section-based notebooks like real books
  • Draw, record, and sync across devices
  • Great for technical students

🗒️ Google Keep – Quick Notes & Reminders

🔗 Download for Android/iOS

  • Sticky note interface
  • Color-coded revision notes
  • Fastest for jotting quick formulas

📚 Anki – Smart Flashcards

🔗 Download for Android/iOS

  • Spaced repetition learning
  • Best for definitions, theory & formulas
  • Custom decks + pre-made options

⏰ Todoist – Daily Planner & Task List

🔗 Download for Android/iOS

  • Create tasks by subject or topic
  • Collaboration features included
  • Track study goals easily

📋 Evernote – Digital Note + File Manager

🔗 Download for Android/iOS/Desktop

  • Attach lecture PDFs & handwritten notes
  • Organize by semester/folder
  • Searchable & sync-ready

📌 Trello – Visual Task Boards

🔗 Download for Android/iOS

  • Kanban-style boards for project tracking
  • Use for group or solo study progress
  • Integrates with Google Drive, Notion

🎧 Study Bunny – Pomodoro + Motivation

🔗 Download for Android/iOS

  • Study timers, coin rewards & fun visuals
  • Tracks your total hours studied
  • Great for bored or distracted students

🔔 Google Calendar – Smart Scheduling

🔗 Download for Android/iOS

  • Plan exam timetables and reminders
  • Sync with Gmail, Zoom, Notion
  • Color-code by subject or topic

🌙 Forest App – Block Phone Distractions

🔗 Download for Android/iOS

  • Study = grow a tree; quit = tree dies
  • Gamified focus booster
  • Great for avoiding Insta/YouTube temptations
