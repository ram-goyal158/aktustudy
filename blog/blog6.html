Important Questions for Data Analytics – AKTU 3rd Year (2025)

Unit-wise Most Asked Questions for B.Tech CSE & IT Students

UNIT 1: Introduction to Data Analytics

  • Define Data Analytics. Explain its types with examples.
  • Differentiate Descriptive, Predictive, and Prescriptive Analytics.
  • What is the Data Analytics life cycle? Explain each phase.
  • Importance of data in business decision-making.
  • Define Big Data and its relation to Data Analytics.

UNIT 2: Data Preprocessing & Visualization

  • Define data preprocessing and explain its steps.
  • What are missing values? Handling techniques?
  • Difference between normalization and standardization.
  • Types of data visualization charts.
  • Short notes on Matplotlib / Seaborn / Tableau.
  • Importance of data cleaning in analysis.

UNIT 3: Statistical Foundations

  • Define mean, median, mode, variance, standard deviation.
  • Explain Pearson and Spearman correlation.
  • What is hypothesis testing? Explain its types.
  • What is p-value? Significance?
  • Parametric vs. non-parametric tests.
  • Explain linear regression with example.

UNIT 4: Data Mining & Clustering

  • What is data mining and its role?
  • Explain k-means clustering with example.
  • Classification vs. clustering.
  • Hierarchical clustering with diagram.
  • Association rule mining: support, confidence, lift.

UNIT 5: Tools & Applications

  • Compare R, Python, Excel for data analysis.
  • Write notes on Hadoop and Spark.
  • Explain MapReduce with workflow.
  • Applications in e-commerce, healthcare, finance.
  • Challenges in real-time data analytics.

Bonus: Frequently Asked University Questions

  • Role of data visualization in decision-making.
  • Difference: supervised vs. unsupervised learning.
  • Python program for linear regression (sklearn).
  • Explain confusion matrix in classification.
  • Dimensionality reduction & PCA explanation.

Preparation Tips for Scoring High

  • Focus more on Units 2, 3, and 4 – higher weightage.
  • Practice numericals and Python-based problems.
  • Revise definitions, diagrams for theory boost.
  • PYQs from last 3 years are highly beneficial.
