Books, Notes, Lab Manuals & Project Ideas

📘 Course Overview – ECE at AKTU

  • 📅 Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)
  • 📚 Subjects: Electronics, Communication, VLSI, Embedded Systems, Signals, and Microprocessors
  • 🧪 Practical: Labs + Mini Projects + Major Project
  • 📝 Exam System: CBCS with Internals, Theory & Viva

📊 Important ECE Labs (AKTU)

  • 3rd–4th Sem: Digital Electronics Lab, Analog Lab
  • 5th Sem: Microprocessors Lab, Signals Lab
  • 6th Sem: Communication Lab, VLSI Design Lab
  • 7th–8th Sem: Embedded Systems Lab, Project Lab

📝 Note: Prepare lab viva questions + diagrams!

🎯 Tips to Score High in AKTU ECE

  • 🔧 Learn basics of PCB design & soldering
