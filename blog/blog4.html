🎯 How to Score 80%+ in AKTU Without Coaching

Complete Self-Study Strategy for Success

Many students believe scoring 80%+ in AKTU is possible only with coaching. But the truth is – you can achieve it with smart self-study, good planning, and the right resources.

In this blog, we’ll show you exactly how to prepare on your own, manage your time, and crack AKTU semester exams without any external tuition or coaching.

📘 1. Understand the AKTU Syllabus (Smartly)

  • Download the latest AKTU syllabus from the official website.
  • Check unit-wise marks weightage.
  • Focus more on high-weightage units.
    Example: If Unit 3 comes every year, master it first.

📚 2. Study Only from Class Notes + Standard Books

  • Don't run after 5 books for one subject.
  • Use class notes, teacher slides/PDFs, and one standard book per subject.
  • Example: Galvin for OS, Balagurusamy for C Programming.

📋 3. Make Your Own Short Notes

  • Create short notes, formulas, flowcharts, and diagrams.
  • These are useful for last-minute revision and memory recall.

📈 4. Solve Previous Year Question Papers (PYQs)

  • Practice at least 3–5 years of PYQs per subject.
  • Questions often repeat with minor changes.
  • Practice writing full answers properly.

⏰ 5. Follow a Realistic Study Time Table

  • No need to study 12 hours a day. Aim for quality 6 hours/day:
    • 2 hrs – Theory
    • 2 hrs – Numericals/Programming
    • 2 hrs – PYQs + Revision
  • Use the Pomodoro Technique (25 mins study + 5 mins break).

🧠 6. Understand, Don’t Cram

  • AKTU values concept clarity more than rote learning.
  • Learn the logic behind formulas and technical content.

✍️ 7. Practice Writing Answers

  • Presentation can help you score bonus marks.
  • Use bullet points, neat diagrams, underlined keywords.

🎥 8. Use Free YouTube & Digital Resources

  • Watch topic-wise AKTU lectures from Easy Engineering Classes, Gate Smashers, etc.
  • Refer NPTEL videos, GeeksforGeeks (for CSE), BYJU’s (for basics).

📊 9. Focus on Internal Marks Too

  • Sessional marks for mid-sems, assignments, and attendance matter.
  • Target 20+ out of 30 internal marks to secure your final percentage.

✅ 10. Revise Smartly in the Last 10 Days

  • Revise short notes, formulas, and repeat questions.
  • Give 1 mock test daily and focus only on known topics.
  • Don’t start anything new at the end!

🏆 Final Words

Self-study + smart strategy = 80%+ easily in AKTU

You don’t need coaching if:

  • You stay consistent
  • You use the right resources
  • You solve PYQs and revise regularly

Believe in yourself. Make a plan. Stick to it. You’ve got this!
