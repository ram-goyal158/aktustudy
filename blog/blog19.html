🎯 AKTU T&P Cell – How to Register & Apply for Jobs (2025 Guide)

Step-by-step process to unlock placement & internship opportunities

🔍 What is the AKTU T&P Cell?

  • Centralized placement hub by AKTU
  • Hosts pool campus & virtual job drives
  • Connects students with MNCs, startups & PSUs

✅ Who Can Register?

  • Final or Pre-final year students (B.Tech, MBA, MCA, etc.)
  • Must belong to AKTU-affiliated institute
  • Should have minimum eligibility as per company criteria
  • Generally, no active backlogs allowed

📝 Documents Required for Registration

  • Passport-size photo (JPG/PNG)
  • Updated Resume (PDF)
  • 10th & 12th Marksheet
  • AKTU Enrollment Number
  • Aadhaar Card
  • College ID

🖥️ How to Register on AKTU T&P Portal

  1. Visit: tp.aktu.ac.in
  2. Click “Student Registration”
  3. Fill your details: Name, Roll Number, Email, Mobile
  4. Upload photo and resume
  5. Submit and verify via OTP/email

📤 How to Apply for Jobs via T&P Portal

  • Login using T&P credentials
  • Go to “Ongoing Drives” or “Job Opportunities”
  • Check eligibility → Click “Apply”
  • Prepare for test/interview

💼 Companies Hiring Through T&P

  • TCS, Infosys, Wipro, HCL, Capgemini
  • Adobe (selected colleges)
  • Startups & MSMEs via pool drives

📅 Types of Placement Drives

Drive TypeDescription
On-campusCompany visits college directly
Off-campusCommon location for many colleges
Virtual drivesOnline recruitment process
Intern + PPOInternship followed by job offer

🧠 Tips to Get Selected

  • Strong resume with projects & certifications
  • Practice aptitude, reasoning, verbal ability
  • Brush up DSA, DBMS, OS for CS/IT students
  • Update LinkedIn & GitHub profiles

🙋 FAQs

Q1: Missed the registration?
Contact your college T&P coordinator.

Q2: Any fees involved?
No. The portal is free for all AKTU students.

Q3: Can 2nd/3rd-year students register?
Only final/pre-final students can apply. Few internship drives are open to 3rd years.

🎯 Final Advice

  • Check portal weekly for new drives
  • Join Telegram or WhatsApp placement groups
  • Apply early and prep smartly
  • Use resources from our [Placement Toolkit]
