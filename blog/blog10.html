🛠️ Mechanical Engineering AKTU (2025)

Subject List, Labs & Project Ideas for ME Students

📚 Course Overview

  • 🎓 Degree: B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
  • 🕒 Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)
  • 📑 Evaluation: Theory + Practical + Project + Viva
  • 📘 Curriculum: CBCS system as per AICTE model

📘 Semester-wise Subject List – ME (AKTU)

✅ 1st Year (Common Subjects)

  • Semester 1: Engineering Physics, Basic Electrical Engineering, Engineering Mathematics I, Communication Skills, Engineering Mechanics, Workshop Practice
  • Semester 2: Engineering Mathematics II, Basic Electronics Engineering, Thermodynamics, Engineering Graphics, Environmental Science, Computer Programming

✅ 2nd Year

  • Semester 3: Material Science & Engineering, Applied Thermodynamics, Mechanics of Solids, Manufacturing Science I, Fluid Mechanics
  • Semester 4: Theory of Machines, Instrumentation & Control, Heat & Mass Transfer, Manufacturing Science II, Fluid Machines, Industrial Engineering

✅ 3rd Year

  • Semester 5: Dynamics of Machines, IC Engines, CAD/CAM, Design of Machine Elements, Refrigeration and Air Conditioning
  • Semester 6: FEM, Power Plant Engineering, Mechanical Vibrations, Mechatronics, Quality Control

✅ 4th Year

  • Semester 7: Industrial Management, Elective I, Project Phase I, Seminar
  • Semester 8: Elective II, Project Phase II, Internship / Viva

🧪 Important Mechanical Labs (AKTU)

SemesterLab Name
3rdMaterial Testing Lab, Thermodynamics Lab
4thFluid Mechanics Lab, Manufacturing Lab
5thDynamics of Machines Lab, CAD Lab
6thHeat Transfer Lab, I.C. Engine Lab
7th/8thRobotics Lab, Vibration Lab, Project Lab

📌 Tip: Maintain neat lab files. AKTU awards internals based on record & viva.

🔬 Mini & Major Project Ideas

🔹 Mini Project Ideas (3rd/5th Sem)

  • Solar Water Heater Model
  • Automatic Car Jack System
  • Simple Hydraulic Arm
  • Gear Train Design
  • Cooling Tower Model

🔹 Major Project Ideas (7th/8th Sem)

  • Hybrid Power Vehicle Model
  • Automatic Grass Cutter using Arduino
  • Heat Exchanger Design
  • Regenerative Braking System
  • Smart Dustbin with Mechatronics
  • Compressed Air Vehicle Prototype

🎯 Use AutoCAD, ANSYS, SolidWorks for design projects.

👉 Choose: B.Tech → Mechanical Engineering

✅ Final Tips for Mechanical Students

  • 🔧 Focus on concept clarity for placements
  • 📝 Solve previous year papers before exams
  • 🎯 Learn software: AutoCAD, ANSYS, SolidWorks
  • 📁 Keep files, notes & projects organized
  • 🤝 Attend fests, tech-events, and project expos
