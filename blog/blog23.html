🌐 Top 10 Websites Every AKTU Student Must Use – 2025 Guide

Boost academics, placements & skill-building with the right online tools

🏛 1. AKTU Official Website

🔗 aktu.ac.in

Get all official notifications like:

  • Exam date sheets, results, and circulars
  • Academic syllabus, revaluation details
  • University holidays & updates

🖥 2. AKTU ERP Portal

🔗 erp.aktu.ac.in

Your main dashboard during exams:

  • Admit card downloads
  • Internal & external marks
  • Exam form filling, profile updates

📚 3. NPTEL (via Swayam)

🔗 swayam.gov.in

Free courses by IITs with certificates:

  • Semester credit-based learning
  • Video lectures + assignments
  • Some courses eligible for AKTU credit transfer

💡 4. GeeksforGeeks

🔗 geeksforgeeks.org

Especially for CSE/IT/MCA students:

  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Placement prep: quizzes, interview Qs
  • Daily coding practice & projects

📖 5. Internshala

🔗 internshala.com

Find internships + learn skills:

  • Remote, part-time, or in-office internships
  • Trainings in Python, Excel, Web Dev, etc.
  • Discounts & tie-ups for AKTU students

📈 6. T&P Cell – AKTU

🔗 tp.aktu.ac.in

Official platform for placements:

  • Campus drive updates & company details
  • Register for T&P drives
  • Get job notices & eligibility criteria

🎯 7. GATE & Govt. Exam Portals

  • GATE 2025 – IIT Kanpur
  • SSC Exams
  • UPSC Exams

For students targeting PSUs, Civil Services, SSC, or Banking.

🧠 8. freeCodeCamp

🔗 freecodecamp.org
  • Learn full-stack development, JavaScript, Python
  • Work on real-world projects
  • 100% free + certified

📥 9. AKTU Study Resource Sites

🔗 aktuStudy.com

Student-made platforms that provide:

  • PYQs (Previous Year Question Papers)
  • Notes, exam tips, syllabus PDFs
  • Branch-wise study guides

📊 10. LinkedIn

🔗 linkedin.com
  • Build your resume profile
  • Connect with HRs, seniors, mentors
  • Showcase certifications, GitHub, projects

✅ Bonus Mentions

Website Purpose
CourseraCourses from top universities

Stack OverflowGet coding doubts solved

Use these sites regularly to build your skills, track AKTU updates, prepare for jobs, and become placement-ready.
